Privacy Policy for Raydex

Raydex is an AI-powered document scanner and OCR app with on-device processing, PDF export, and AdMob-supported advertising. This statement explains how your data is handled when using the app via Google Play Store.

Effective date: May 17, 2026

Welcome to Raydex. This Privacy Policy explains how Raydex collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Raydex Android app distributed through Google Play Store.

1. What this app does

Raydex is an on-device document scanner and OCR app that converts camera images into searchable documents and PDF files. The app offers document scanning, image editing, OCR text recognition, and multi-page PDF export.

2. On-device processing

Raydex processes all scanned documents locally on your device. Document images, OCR text, and PDF files are processed and stored on the device only. We do not upload your scanned images or text to any external server for processing.

3. Information we collect

Raydex is designed to collect minimal data. The app does not require registration, and it does not collect personal account information.

3.1 Data collected for app functionality

  • Camera access: required only to scan physical documents.
  • Storage access: required to save scanned documents, exported PDFs, and images on your device.
  • Local OCR data: the text recognized from your documents remains on your device.

3.2 Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect your scanned document content.
  • We do not collect GPS location, contacts, calendar entries, call logs, or microphone audio.
  • We do not require an account, email address, or phone number.

4. Ads and third-party services

Raydex is an ad-supported free app that displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect limited data such as advertising identifiers and usage signals to provide personalized and non-personalized ads.

This app does not control how ad partners use the data collected by Google AdMob. For details, please refer to Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Possible third-party ad-related data may include:

  • Device identifiers and Google advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App usage and interactions with ads
  • Anonymous analytics and performance data

Note: If you choose to click an ad, the privacy practices of the ad source apply. Please review their privacy statements separately.

5. Google Play permissions

Raydex may request the following permissions:

  • Camera – to scan physical documents.
  • Storage / Files and media – to save scanned images, PDF exports, and documents on your device.

6. Analytics and crash reporting

Raydex does not collect personally identifiable analytics by default. If analytics or crash reporting are enabled, they will be limited to anonymous usage metrics and error reports needed to improve app stability.

7. Children

Raydex is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us and we will take prompt action.

8. Data retention

All scanned documents, recognized text, and exported PDFs remain on your device until you delete them. We do not retain or store your scans on our servers.

9. Your rights

You may withdraw consent for permission use by uninstalling the app or revoking permissions from your Android settings. Since Raydex does not maintain user accounts, there is no account data to request or delete from our servers.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with a revised effective date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact the app developer through the Play Store listing or email us at support@raydexapp.com.
